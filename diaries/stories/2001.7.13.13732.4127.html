A Troll (5.00 / 1) (#9)
by bc on Fri Jul 13th, 2001 at 02:54:58 PM PST
A troll is someone posting lies he does not believe in an effort to provoke inflamed discussion, discussion where rationality goes out of the window and the posters succumb to their dogma.

Adequacy.org may post controversial material, but we do so in an effort to provoke good discussion and also in a spirit of humour (some article are intended to be funny, and yet have a serious point at their heart, where others are totally serious).

We intend to challenge all dogmas and principles that people take to heart, yes, but we are totally opposed to trolling nonetheless.

You can consider this site to be a place where outrageous ideas are beta tested and established ideas attacked. The distinction between trolling and this is fine, perhaps, but I believe it is there.
