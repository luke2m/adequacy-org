Deletion Notice
In this diary entry, I encourage the upstanding members of Adequacy to think deeply on the problem of script-kiddism, and share their thoughts.

It is only a matter of time, I fear, before another attack. How should we prepare for this eventuality? What should be done to the offender? See Poll.

Known script kiddies will not be permitted to post comments in this diary entry. Any such comments will be deleted. Unfortunately, but obviously, this means that anonymous commentary can not be permitted. Comments by Anonymous Reader will therefore be deleted as well.

There are some people who have a great deal of trouble understanding that they have to play by the rules. This, on its own, might simply be the consequence of a rebellious outlook.

If, however, these same people cry "foul!" with great vehemence when their schemes are foiled, their ideation can no longer be interpreted as mere rebellion; it is, instead, the very essence of the selfish criminal mindset.

Be vigilant.
