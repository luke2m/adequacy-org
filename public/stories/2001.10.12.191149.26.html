Well, tonight I went out with my folks to a restaurant. We do this *every* friday, and I'm not one to complain about it, since they usually take me to decent places... Anyhow, tonight we went to a joint called Famous Sam's. It's a sports bar/restaurant dealie, and the atmosphere *seemed* friendly enough; the food was ok (we had wings and their fish fry, which was extraoardinarily greasy), and our waitress wouldn't stop staring at me for some reason (I think she wanted my nuts). I ordered the fish fry, and my mum and dad got some buffalo wings. It was all fine except for the fact that I recieved two small pieces of fish and when I asked for new ones, it took them 35 minutes to prepare 'em. Talk about frustrating. By the time my food came out, I was no longer hungry, which completely defeated the purpose of bringing out the food...and if I had my way, our waitress' tip would've been reduced by 4% just out of spite. Bah...

And as another day ends...
And I get to see Lexx tonight. Yeehaw!

That show has been the most impressive, IMO, over the years. It's got the perfect mix of cheesy acting, creative scenery, rather imaginative plots and expendable extras. :) I mean, there's really very little, I think, that anyone can do to improve upon it...it's the one show I always try to watch every week. I like the concept of having a small crew of 3 'people' who are so wildly different from each other, each being deficient in certain things (Kai being dead, Stanley being a fool/coward, and Xev being a whore), while others in the group seem to more than make up for those deficiencies. It seems to make the show seem a bit more interesting than it might be if everyone in it was as normal as, say, you or me (in fact, a show about me would probably put everyone to sleep..heh). I like it much more than that Farscape show (which was really, really cruddy early on, unlike Lexx, which had some really awesome pilots), and all the other crappy shows they have on the SciFi channel (excepting Quantum Leap, which is OK). And that's enough rambling for now, I suppose...don't flame me for it, please ;)
