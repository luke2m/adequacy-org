I'd be a ...

by cheetah

If brains were fridges,
I'd be a washing machine.

If couches were potatoes,
I'd be a stick of celery.

If monkeys were bananas,
I'd be an opposable thumb.

If the world was an oyster,
that'd be really dumb.
