Girlfriend is better

She's a psychotic bitch. She's mean spirited, argumentative and too smart for her own good. All the qualities I find most desirable in a woman really.

Physically she's great. She wears a size 6 shoe and has perfectly formed feet. Back in the day she used to let me play with them.

Problem is that she's mormon and we kind of parted on bad terms (it's been a couple of years though).
