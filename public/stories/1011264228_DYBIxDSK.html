Chinese water torture on the other hand aims to first inflict a long period of agony and not really pain - and then drive the victim insane.

The victim is restrained and has continual drops of water fall on their forehead in intervals of 20-30 seconds. Eventually the drops start to feel louder and louder as the victim starts "losing it". The drops are also apparently supposed to hurt as well after extended ammounts of time as bruising develops.
All this means that the victim is constantly dreading the innevitable next drop which eventually drives them insane.

Another nice trick is to not give the victim any water - this way they will be thirsty and constantly reminded of the fact by the drops falling on them but unable to reach them.
