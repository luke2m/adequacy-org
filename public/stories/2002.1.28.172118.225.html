So, Aphasia Networks doesn't have too many machines being used right now. We've got Adequacy, and a couple other weblogs. There's a few low low traffic static web sites, a shell server, and a low traffic shared box. Oh, and there's our Return to Castle Wolfenstein server, which uses about 180 kB/s. So, our total traffic should be around 250 kB at most. But, the switch is showing over 3 mB/s. That's a huge difference. So, I have to figure out what is wrong with my mrtg, and what is using all our bandwidth. It should be easy to first go about firewalling off all the machines and limit them to a certain amount of bandwidth. Then, I'll be able to raise the limits on machines that may need it. It shouldn't be too difficult.
