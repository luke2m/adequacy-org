I won't mention the name of the hot deals forum that I frequent unless you ask me, because I think that's the right thing to do. But let me say, I'm past the point where I can help myself.

More diaries by FreemoreJohnson

At least I'm not yet at the point of some of the helpless freaks there who have maxed out five or six credit cards, but I've lost something maybe more precious: All of my time. Aside from this place, I spend all of my time refreshing the page, hoping for a new link to some survey where I can acquire another t-shirt from some soulless company, or find a $20 cd-burner or a $400 dollar video card for only $150.

The real tragedy of it is that in searching for deals, we just impoverish ourselves.

I admit that I am addicted to hot deals and cannot help myself. There, I've said it.
