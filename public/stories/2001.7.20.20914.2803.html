Once again for those in the back (5.00 / 3) (#4)
by zikzak on Sat Jul 21st, 2001 at 02:12:32 AM PST
Adequacy is not Kuro5hin or Slashdot. It is also not a sand box for adolescents - or those who just act like children - to wallow in while calling each other names and looking to find a present left behind by the neighbor's cat.

This is an edited site. That means we, the editors, will edit content that we consider to be in need of editing by our editorial staff.

Adequacy is no different than the New York Times or the Wall Street Journal. We choose which stories to publish. We decide which commentary from our readers is fit for publication. We dictate the nature of the site.

Your diary is a privlege, not a right. Anything posted to it may be adjusted by any editorial staff member at any time for any reason. Your diary and comment posting privleges may be revoked if there is compelling reason to do so. These privleges may also be revoked for no apparent reason whatsoever.

There is also no official editorial policy governing this site of any sort. Editors may choose to agree or disagree with the actions, comments, and interpretations of any other editor. I speak for the entire staff when I say that I do not have the right to speak for any of the staff.

Thank you for choosing Adequacy. Please enjoy your stay.
