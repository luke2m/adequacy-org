Yes there are serious articles and piss-take articles here. Some people critise other sites and some other sites critise them. But i think that shouldnt be in it, unless you really think so.

Adequacy is really good for stories and information and stuff. It suceeds where all other sites fail..but is it just all of it or is there another, deeper dark part to adequacy?

I think there was something put which made us not allowed to question anything..or anyone here for that matter. But regardless of that I must try to at least attempt to try and sum it up. Because who else will try do it too?

I think its been about 5 months since I got here.. because it was less than before doesnt mean that I cant speak about it at all..quite the opposite of that - if you know what I mean.

Maybe some people here think its not actually here for something but I think that the other sites are missing a bit of it really. When I say that, I mean that it isnt what could be done without proper thought. Of course some people would argue - that based on the earlier assumption that it might not have succeeded as they predicted. Well, that's just opinion and while opinion might be fact if its not in the context of whats here it might also not be whats really important.

The one thing noticble about adequacy is the depth of itself compared to other ones. I imagined that it came of chance but now I think it is a far more mysterious thing. Like why exactly cant we figure out why? We are here for a reason yes or no? We dont know - thats simply put but not a direct way to solve a piece of it. Another point is there is SOMETHING in the background. I dont know what, but its alive in some way which isnt like we think of. Its not so much controling as created by all of us. Maybe its just a lot of it in the same area. Afterall, haven't we seen many of it before in other places? Im sure thats true..at least I hope. The alternative is very dire. Whats not true is what really goes on - the sum of its parts...which is us. Unless of course you think that we aren't parts but that we are just part of something else. That's going off topic though.

So to sum it up - I dont believe it is possible to fully understand Adequacy. So would people stop trying..not that they can't have the right to thinking it as is happening here..I dont want to destroy my own post I think...but I dont know if thats covered by what Ive said. hmmm interesting.
