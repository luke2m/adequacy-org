My solution? Don't give them the dignity of your consideration. Don't consider their opinions, their beliefs or even their existance worth your time. In short, consider those who demonstrate that they are willing to think of others as less than human as subhuman themselves.

I realize that not only is adopting such a mindset difficult, but that it has significant moral implications as well. My answer to most of these concerns is still to respect (I almost said value, but that's not quite right) the rights of these beings, disregarding only the fruits of the ways in which they exercise these rights. I've succesfully used this approach for the last eight years, and it's kept me quite sane (though, admittedly, I haven't really been targeted by anyone within the last three... not that I've noticed, anyhow). By determining my value based only on the opinions of those I respect, I've become a much happier man. Perhaps 'twould be better if my self-respect were internally generated, but that's a whole 'nother discussion there.

Or you could move to Kuro5hin -- it's not quite so bad there.

Btw, I'm not at my most optimistic wrt human nature at the moment myself, having just been sorely used by someone I care deeply about; I hope it doesn't seep into my post. Ahh, well... life sucks, eh? :)
