osm was completely unaware of the nubile form approaching from the deep mists of the forest and was rather startled at the soft, sweet voice, "hello!"

osm turned to the hypnotic eyes which ensnared his gaze. he completely forgot about the itching clump of moist fur, "uh-hhhhh-huh--hhhhhh," gasped osm.

"i am opalhawk! i am hot and young and my womb is fertile," purred the vision.

osm composed himself, at the expense of a severe outbreak of perspiration, "i am osm the otter. if you would be so kind as to remove this matted fur from my buttocks, i shall be ever in your debt!"

opalhawk smiled kindly at the poor creature as she removed a machete from a sheath fastened to her ankle. carefully, caringly, she cut the parasitic lump of fur from osm's tail-base. osm's hind leg quivered uncontrollably. opalhawk smiled again and scratched the bald spot left by the removal of the clump. osm's head waivered wildly and he licked at the air.

"now, you must help me, as a token of your immeasurable gratitude, mr. otter."

"let us have a picnic in the park," opalhawk said, inflecting her voice to indicate she may be asking more than suggesting.

osm thought a moment, "a picnic would be lovely!"
