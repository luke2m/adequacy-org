Whatever it is you believe, I assure you you there is something you can learn here. And you may not like it, though I can't tell for sure.

Update [2001-7-10 17:5:45 by elby]: Edited for clarity and content.

Pet owners are vile, disgusting pigs. Linux is for maladjusted freaks. Video games turn kids into killers, and, in some cases, plumbers. White middle class men and corporations are the purest evil that exists.

These are all claims that have been made on this site. Truly my eyes have been opened to opinions I had never though of before. Much of it has been far more level headed and reasoned than anything I've contributed to the site.

Why is it that we, as humans, and especially as internet users, find the need to express out viewpoints so vehemently that there is no room for debate? Why is it that we have such a drive to arrive at an answer, and when one is arrived at, we defend it so firmly that any challenge to it is blasphemous and any alternative is wrong? It truly is time for a site like adequacy.org

Thank you.
