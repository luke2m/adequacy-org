I am at the tender age of nineteen at which I must seriously consider what I want to do with the rest of my life. For many years, I had been unable to find a career that was adequate with my hedonistic lifestyle. It seemed that every career upon which I came required me either to leave my home environment daily or to seclude myself from the populous. I also needed a career that would give me a chance to help others. I knew my college years would not last forever, and that suicide would be my option if I could not find the perfect career. Wallowing in my sorrow, I turned to the only thing that could help me in times of need: bishoujo games. At last I finally found the perfect career for me!
