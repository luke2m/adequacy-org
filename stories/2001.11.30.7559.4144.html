To set the record straight
  • Oh I tried, yes I did. I thought this was the place for me, but it would seem that I was gravely mistaken. You see, I thought a nuclear rocket scientist would be welcome at a webiste which caters towards bright people, but I've met nothing but resistance and flames here. You can all piss off and rot in heck you sorry little tarts. Some of you dolts had the nerve to suggest I have a drug/alcohol issue, some of you have flamed my Southern Baptist faith and most of you have simply made inane remarks to my freely given content. Well.

    So, I dont' need your flames and I'm going to continue on my quest of searching for a website where I'll be welcome and where I'll fit in. Don't try to convince me to stay, don't waste your time filling my diary up with pleas because it's too late. Once I make my mind up about something it's a done deal.

  • Quite frankly it's with a heavy heart that I submit my "I'm leaving" diary, I really had high hopes for my future here. Alas, I guess the road to hell is paved with good intentions. So, all you little buggers can find someone else to flame because your old pal Syslvester won't be here for you to pick on. In the back of my mind I secretly know you are all just jelous of my intellect and my supreme talents, so I can at least take that knowledge with me. I do hope that you people have the maturity to overcome your petty issues and treat the next people who come to this site with the respect they deserve. In the meantime you can rest assuered that I will no longer subject myself to the nebulous dribble that you insufferable trollops continue to spew.

    Good Day.
