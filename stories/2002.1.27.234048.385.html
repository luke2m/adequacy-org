Wicca is an ancient religion.

Wrong. Wicca came into existence in the 60's during the rise of the feminist movement. Adopting its ideals from Lord of the Rings and Dungeons and Dragons, it was designed to be a politically correct religion by women, for women.

Wiccans are peaceful and loving people.

Wrong. Wiccans are peaceful toward other Wiccans and Satanists, but they are extremely hostile toward those who question their beliefs.

I know from experience that Wiccans are the most deceitful and pretentious people on Earth. One would be wiser to trust even the notorious Gypsies.

Wiccans can do magic.

Stop believing in fairy tales.

Wiccans do not believe they follow the one true religion.

That is a lie. They constantly state that there is a God and a Goddess, implying that the beliefs of Christians, stating that there is only one God, are wrong. Wiccans merely state that they do not believe their religion to be the one true religion because they are liars.

Wiccans are usually beautiful Gothic women.

The average Wiccan tends to be morbidly obese.

Wicca is not an organized religion.

Although Wicca likes to boast about its disorganization, it is clear to the intellectual that Wicca is indeed a very strict religion. To be a true Wiccan, one must follow the Wiccan Rede, which is a lengthy text describing the many traits one must adopt before she is a true Wiccan. To be a Christian, one must simply accept Jesus Christ as his/her Lord and Savior and live an honest Christian lifestyle. One can plainly see that Wicca demands more confirming to one standard than Christianity.

Hopefully now, the misinformation spreading of Wicca can cease and desist.
