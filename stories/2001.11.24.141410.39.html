Oh luisa, who are you?

More diaries by legolas
Playing with fire
Through the cloud of anonymity, you taunt me.
You have rated me 4,
And I don't know what I have done
to earn your affection.

Are you the woman of my dreams?
Are you an Afghan woman, shedding her veil for the first time?
Are you an artist, unleashing her vision on the world?

Did Kuro5hin lose your way?
Did CNN break your heart?
Did video kill the radio star?

Or, like so many visions of love before,
are you a farce?
Are you a man?
or worse still,
are you Laura?

Oh, lusia, why don't you tell me who you are?
Oh, lusia, why do you taunt me so?
Oh, luisa... i need to know.
