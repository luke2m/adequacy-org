I'm struggling here. I lurked for a couple weeks, got a feel for things, made a diary entry and got it deleted. Gradually, I saw the points from which people were arguing, how they were arguing, and what the popular motifs for comments and diary entries were. I did my best to become the best little Adequacy Bot I could be. People were starting to write well-thought-out responses to my comments. I even hit a gold mine: I spawned a large thread with little relavance toward my original comment, and even some biblical prooftexting. Yet there is a problem.
