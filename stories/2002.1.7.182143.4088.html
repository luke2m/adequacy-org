 Linux Zealot goes to the Movies

 Author:
mjl
 Topic:
Linux Zealot
 Posted:
Jan 21, 2002
 Comments:

Linux Zealot likes to take a break from re-inventing the wheel by going to the movies.

While normal people are happy to watch just about anything, Linux Zealot sticks to movies that he is comfortable with. Linux Zealot enjoys the movies that the rest of us ignore, for example Hackers, Pirates of Silicon Valley, and The Net.

Linux Zealot is taking Linux Zealot Girlfriend to see The Lord of the Rings and is very excited by the use of free software to create the special effects in the movie. In this strip, Linux Zealot learns many important lessons, including the true value of so-called free software.
