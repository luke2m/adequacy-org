Pi: Most people think pi (3.1415926) has no tangible pattern. I, being the skeptic, believe that it is the slight opposite of infinite- merely extremely large. I believe that the impossible is sometimes possible so indeed there is an algorithm out there that could decypher pi into a pattern. But still- could it be Infinite? The Universe: Is there truly an unlimited amount of stars and space in our universe? Well, most certainly there has to be a large amount considering the tiny odds of a life-holding planet to be born (and evolve sentient life) but Infinite seems far fetched. Dimensions: I believe that the amount of dimensions probably mirrors The Universe, but to a extreme level. It is possible there could be more than 99e999999 dimensions out there, but then again there could also be Infinite Timeline: This is the doozy. Is there an end of time? Is there a beginning. Is the Big Bang and Big Crunch a recurring event? Does history repeat itself? It is my belief that time travels in a circle- never stopping, never ceasing. But at what time can time not exist or the laws nullified?
