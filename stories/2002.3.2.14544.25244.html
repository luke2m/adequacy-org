It depends. (none / 0) (#3)

I think $100/night might be possible. Have a look at MAUI.NET. I would recommend Kaanapali beach hotel having stayed there myself, but you're looking at $165/night rack rate or about $130 if you can get a discounted rate since it is high season.

A search of travelnow shows 2-star accomodation at around $80/night. So it depends how much luxury you require.

Maui is a really amazing island. There's lots of things to do, the people are very friendly, the scenery is amazing. I wholeheartedly recommend it.
